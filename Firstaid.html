First Aid Assistance

Your emergency guide until help arrives.

Bleeding Burns Fractures Heart Attacks Snake Bites Drowning Choking Electric Shock

🩸 Bleeding

  • ✅ Apply firm pressure using a clean cloth.
  • ✅ Keep the injured part elevated if possible.
  • ✅ Seek medical attention if bleeding doesn't stop.
  • ❌ Do not remove objects stuck in the wound.

🔥 Burns

  • ✅ Cool the burn under running water for 10–20 minutes.
  • ✅ Cover the burn with a clean, non-stick bandage.
  • ✅ Seek medical help if the burn is large or severe.
  • ❌ Do not apply ice or oily substances like butter.

🤕 Fractures

  • ✅ Keep the injured area still and supported.
  • ✅ Apply a splint if trained to do so.
  • ✅ Seek medical attention immediately.
  • ❌ Do not move the broken bone unnecessarily.

💔 Heart Attacks

  • ✅ Call emergency services immediately.
  • ✅ Help the person sit and stay calm.
  • ✅ Loosen tight clothing and reassure them.
  • ✅ If trained, begin CPR if the person becomes unresponsive.

🐍 Snake Bites

  • ✅ Keep the victim calm and still to slow the spread of venom.
  • ✅ Keep the bitten limb below heart level.
  • ✅ Seek immediate medical attention.
  • ❌ Do not suck the venom or apply ice.

🌊 Drowning

  • ✅ Remove the person from water safely.
  • ✅ Check for breathing and pulse.
  • ✅ Begin CPR if the person is unresponsive.
  • ✅ Call for emergency help immediately.

🤧 Choking

  • ✅ Ask if the person can cough or speak.
  • ✅ If not, give 5 back blows followed by 5 abdominal thrusts.
  • ✅ Repeat until the object is dislodged or they become unconscious.
  • ✅ If unconscious, start CPR and call for emergency help.

⚡ Electric Shock

  • ✅ Turn off the power source if safe to do so.
  • ✅ Call emergency services immediately.
  • ✅ Check for breathing and pulse; begin CPR if needed and trained.
  • ✅ Treat any visible burns with clean, dry cloths.
  • ❌ Do not touch the person until the electricity is turned off.

This guide is for informational purposes only. For serious injuries, contact emergency services immediately.
